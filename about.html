Who is Stefan?

My name is Stefan Trajanovski, a 20-year-old content writer, graphic designer, and computer science student from the charming town of Resen, Macedonia. I’m currently studying at the Faculty of Computer Science and Engineering in Skopje, combining my creative background with a strong foundation in technology.
With years of experience as a freelancer on Upwork, I’ve developed a reliable reputation in content writing and graphic design, and I’ve expanded my skill set into the world of development. I’m now well-versed in C++, Java, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, React, and even low-level programming with MIPS, allowing me to approach projects with both technical precision and creative insight.
Punctuality and professionalism are values I live by—I understand the importance of deadlines and strive to deliver top-tier work on time, every time. I offer unlimited revisions, ensuring my clients are 100% satisfied with the final result. I take pride in being a perfectionist, dedicated to capturing and executing every detail of a client’s vision.
Motivated by curiosity and a desire to grow, I embrace new challenges that push me to improve and evolve. Whether it’s a creative task or a complex technical project, I approach every opportunity with focus, commitment, and care. Thank you for considering me for your project. I look forward to collaborating with you and bringing your ideas to life. Let’s create something exceptional together.
